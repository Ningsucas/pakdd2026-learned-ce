# Reading List

This list collects selected references related to the PAKDD 2026 tutorial on learning-based cardinality estimation.

## Foundations and Query Optimization

- Leis et al. How Good Are Query Optimizers, Really? VLDB 2015.
- Ioannidis. The History of Histograms. VLDB 2003.
- Chaudhuri. An Overview of Query Optimization in Relational Systems. PODS 1998.

## Relational Learned Cardinality Estimation

- Kipf et al. Learned Cardinalities: Estimating Correlated Joins with Deep Learning. CIDR 2019.
- Yang et al. Deep Unsupervised Cardinality Estimation. VLDB 2019.
- Hilprecht et al. DeepDB: Learn from Data, not from Queries! VLDB 2020.
- Sun and Li. End-to-End Learning-Based Query Optimization. SIGMOD 2019.

## Graph Cardinality Estimation

- Park et al. Cardinality Estimation over Knowledge Graphs with Embeddings and Graph Neural Networks.
- Kim et al. Combining Sampling and Synopses with Worst-Case Optimal Runtime and Quality Guarantees for Graph Pattern Cardinality Estimation.
- Zhao et al. Graph-based cardinality estimation methods.

## Multi-model and Cross-model Cardinality Estimation

- Deng et al. MANN: Multi-head Attention Network Model for Estimating Cardinality in Multi-model Databases.
- Papers on multi-model benchmarks, cross-model query processing, and learned estimation will be added.
